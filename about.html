Hi, my name is Isabella Burns! With this website I wanted to discover a few things. The title of this site is “My Spotify Archive” to highlight that this is personal. While being personal, I want this to be a reason to push its audience to do some exploring of their own listening habits.

The “Home” page displays an image of a playlist I have recently made and have been listening to non-stop. It also has a few images of album covers from songs I frequently listen to.

The “Semester 1” page has images of the album covers of the songs I listened to the most from late August to mid December. It also contains a description of what those songs prove about my first semester.

The “Semester 2” page has the same layout and concept just fit for my listening habits from mid January to early May.

I really hope you enjoyed what I discovered about myself, and use this to fuel your imagination and think about how your music listening habits reflect your life, especially core moments.
